Server Rules

  • Be respectful and friendly to other members. No bullying, harassment, or discrimination of any kind.
  • Use appropriate language and content. No profanity, NSFW, or illegal content.
  • Stay on topic and follow the channel guidelines. No spamming, off-topic, or irrelevant messages.
  • Do not advertise or promote other servers, products, or services without permission. No self-promotion or begging.
  • Do not abuse or exploit any bots, commands, or features. No cheating, hacking, or glitching.
  • Follow the Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. No raiding, doxxing, impersonation, or other malicious behavior.
  • Listen to the staff and follow their instructions. No arguing, disrespecting, or ignoring the staff.
  • Have fun and enjoy your time on the server. No drama, toxicity, or negativity.
